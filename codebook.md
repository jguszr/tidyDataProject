## Objective

This code book summarizes the resulting data fields in `tidy.txt`.

## Identifiers

* `subject` - The test subject ID
* `activity` - The activity performed when getting the  measurements

## Labels by Activity

* `WALKING` (value *1*): subject was walking while on test
* `WALKING_UPSTAIRS` (value *2*): subject was walking up on a  staircase during the test
* `WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS` (value *3*): subject was walking down on a  staircase during the test
* `SITTING` (value *4*): subject was sitting during the test
* `STANDING` (value *5*): subject was standing during the test
* `LAYING` (value *6*): subject was laying down during the test
